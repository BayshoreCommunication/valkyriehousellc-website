Who We Are

Built on lived experience.
Led by women, for women.

Valkyrie House is more than a place to live. It is a home where every woman is met with understanding — and given the tools to begin again.

01

Our Mission

To provide a safe, supportive, and empowering environment for women in recovery — fostering healing, accountability, and personal growth. We are committed to helping each woman rebuild her life with dignity, purpose, and lasting sobriety.

02

Our Vision

To create a thriving community where women in recovery are given the opportunity to heal, rediscover their strength, and lead purposeful, self-sufficient lives — becoming a trusted foundation for long-term transformation and generational impact.

Why Valkyrie House

A home — not just a house.

At Valkyrie House, every detail is designed to support real recovery. Structure that brings peace. Accountability that builds confidence. A community of women who understand — because they have walked it too.

We believe every woman has the strength to rise again. Our home offers a safe and empowering environment where you can heal, rediscover who you are, and build a life rooted in purpose, stability, and long-term sobriety.

What We Offer

Everything you need to rebuild.
